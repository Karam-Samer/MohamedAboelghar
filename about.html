About Prof. Dr. Mohamed Aboelghar

Biography

Professor, Head of Agricultural Applications, Soil and Marine Sciences Division, National Authority for Remote Sensing and Space Sciences (NARSS) starting June 2025. Obtained PhD in Remote Sensing from Chiba University, Japan (March 2005) and Master’s in Applications of Remote Sensing in Agriculture from Menofia University (1998). Previously Researcher (2001-2005), Lecturer at Center for Environmental Remote Sensing (CEReS), Chiba University (2005-2008), Researcher at NARSS (2008-2013), and Head of Agricultural Applications Department (2013-2025).

Worked as Visiting Professor at Zewail City of Science and Technology, Menofia University, and Ain Shams University. Served as Remote Sensing and GIS technical expert and trainer for Arab Organization for Agricultural Development (2015). Principal Investigator and team member in many projects involved in vegetation monitoring, crop yield prediction, land cover mapping, and assessment of crop diseases using satellite data. Organized and joined many training courses and programs for remote sensing and GIS teaching.
